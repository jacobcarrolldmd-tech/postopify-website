iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open in Safari (not Chrome)

  5. Close Safari & reopen from the Home Screen icon for alarms to work
Android (Chrome)
  1. Open in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap "Add" — done!
