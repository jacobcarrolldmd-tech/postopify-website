Backup reminders

Setting up Telegram reminders.

Browser notifications work great while your phone is unlocked and the app stays open in the background. Telegram is an optional backup for dose reminders that need to reach you even when your phone is locked or the browser has been closed.

This app uses the free Telegram app for extra-reliable reminders even when your phone is locked.

Don’t want another app? Browser reminders in Postopify still work fine on their own — Telegram is optional.

Do I have to use Telegram?
No. It's entirely optional. Browser reminders in Postopify work on their own — Telegram just adds a backup channel that can still reach you when your phone is locked or the app isn't open.
Is Telegram safe to use?
Telegram is a free, widely used messaging app. Postopify only uses it to send you dose-time text reminders — it doesn't have access to your medical information, and you can disconnect it at any time by blocking the reminder bot in Telegram.
How do I stop Telegram reminders once I'm done with my medications?
Simply block the Postopify reminders bot in Telegram, or don't reconnect it the next time you use the app.
General questions

Everything else, answered.

Common questions about using the Postopify patient app day to day.

Do I need to download an app?
No. Postopify opens right in your phone's browser when you scan your practice's QR code. You can optionally add it to your Home Screen so it behaves like an app, but nothing needs to be downloaded from an app store.
Is my information private?
Your dose schedule and progress are stored on your own device, not on a central server. If you choose to connect Telegram for backup reminders, only the dose times and medication names needed to send you a reminder are used — nothing else.
What happens if I lose my phone or clear my browser data?
Since your schedule is saved on your device, clearing your browser data or switching phones will reset it. Ask your practice for a new QR code and you can quickly re-enter your dose times.
Why didn't I get a reminder?
The most common reasons are notification permissions not being enabled, or the reminder not being re-scheduled after midnight (reminders reset daily — open the app each morning to confirm today's times). Connecting Telegram as a backup helps cover both of these cases.
Can I track more than one patient on the same phone?
Yes. Postopify supports multiple patient profiles on the same device, so a parent or caregiver managing more than one person's recovery can switch between them.
Who do I contact with questions about my actual medications or recovery?
Postopify is a reminder and instruction tool, not a substitute for medical advice. For questions about your prescriptions, symptoms, or recovery, please contact your practice directly.
