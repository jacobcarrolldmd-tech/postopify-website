Why paper fails

A discharge sheet does its job for one ride home.

After that, it's folded into a bag, left on a nightstand, or gone — right when a patient most needs to remember which medication comes at 2 PM. Postopify replaces the sheet with a link every phone already knows how to open, so the instructions are still there on day three, not just day one.

How it works

Four steps, no code changes.

01

Add your procedures

Drop a Google Doc into your practice's folder for each procedure. The title becomes the name patients see.

02

Hand out your QR code

Each practice gets one code, branded with your clinic's name, logo, and colors.

03

Patient scans, no app

The code opens a page built for their procedure: instructions plus a medication schedule they can check off.

04

Update anytime

Edit the Google Doc and the change is live immediately. Nothing to redeploy, nothing to reprint.
